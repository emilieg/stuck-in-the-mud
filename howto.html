Game Rules

The first player rolls all five dice. If any 2s or 5s are rolled, no points are scored for this throw. If no 2s or 5s are rolled, the total of the dice will be added to your score.

The player sets aside any 2s and 5s ("stuck in the mud"), and throws the remaining dice. Again, if any 2s or 5s are thrown, no points are scored. If there are no 2s or 5s, the total will be added to the previous score.

Continue in this way until all the dice are Stuck in the Mud. After the score is totaled, play passes to the next player. There are a total of 3 rounds of play.
